About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Operations Research and Information Engineering at Cornell University, where I am very fortunate to be advised by Professor Damek Davis. Prior to graduate school, I obtained my B.S. from the Department of Mathematics at Nanjing University in 2019. In 2018, I spent one year as a visiting student in the Department of Mathematics at University of Wisconsin-Madison.

My research interest lies in the intersection of optimization, geometry, and machine learning.

Outside of research, I am an avid (rock) climber. I have just sent the Illustrious Buddha (V8) at Gunks!
